Strawberry Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 350g Strawberries
  • 600g Bananas
  • 3L MilK
  • 600g Sugar

Procedure:

  1. Join the Strawberries, bananas and sugar and mix them all
  2. In another bowl, mix the milk
  3. Add them together
  4. Put them in the mixer
  5. Remove them from mixer, into a cold recipient and put in blast freezer for 10 minutes

Notes:

  • Before miximg the chocolate, melt it in the microwave for intervals of 5 minutes
  • After removing from the machine, add chocolate and mix the ice cream
